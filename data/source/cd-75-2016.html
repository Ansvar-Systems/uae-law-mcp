قرار رقم 75 لسنة 2016
صادر بتاريخ 29/9/2016م.
بشأن تحديد الرسوم والتكاليف التي يستوفيها المُرخّص لهم نظير مشاركة المواقع الداخلية للهاتف المتحرّك في الدولة
رئيس مجلس إدارة الهيئة العامّة لتنظيم قطاع الاتصالات،
بعد الاطلاع على المرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (3) لسنة 2003م في شأن تنظيم قطاع الاتصالات ولائحته التنفيذية وتعديلاتهما.
وعلى القرار رقم (20) لسنة 2010م بشأن الموافقة على الرسوم المقترحة للمشاركة في مواقع الهاتف المتحرّك.
وبناءً على ما عرضه مدير عام الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الاتصالات وموافقة مجلس الإدارة.
قررنا ما يلي:
المادة الأولى-
تُحدّد الرسوم والتكاليف التي يستوفيها المُرخّص لهم نظير مشاركة المواقع الداخلية للهاتف المتحرك في دولة الإمارات العربية المتحدة. وفقاً للأحكام والضوابط والجدول المُبيّن في الوثيقة المُرفقة بهذا القرار.
المادة 2-
على المُرخّص لهم الالتزام بالأحكام والضوابط وقيم الرسوم والتكاليف المُبيّنة في الوثيقة المُرفقة بهذا القرار، بالإضافة إلى الالتزامات المنصوص عليها في المادة (41) من المرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (3) لسنة 2003 المُشار إليه، وعدم القيام بأي فعل من شأنه الإخلال بتلك الأحكام والضوابط.
المادة 3-
يُعمل بهذا القرار من تاريخ صدوره ويُنشر في الجريدة الرسمية.

1- Legal Background
1.1 Article 12 of Federal Law by Decree No. (3) of 2003 as amended (“the Law”) established the TRA as the, “... competent body to oversee the telecommunications sector in the State and all Licensees...”
1.2 Article 14 (1) and (2) of the Law specifically grants to the TRA the power to issue regulations, instructions, decisions and rules regulating the following:
a) “... tariffs, charges and fees levied by Licensees...”;
b) “... anything related to the use and Interconnection of Telecommunications Networks and Telecommunications Services provided by the Licensees, co-location and sharing of infrastructure by such Licensees, including the special conditions for costs of such Interconnection, access, site-sharing, time-scales and principles for negotiations and completion of agreements between concerned entities in relation to the foregoing matters”.
1.3 Articles 2.14 and 2.15 of the TRA’s Mobile Site Sharing Instructions Version 1.0 (the “Mobile Site Sharing Instructions”), stipulate that the charges levied between Licensees for site sharing “... shall be based on reasonable and efficiently incurred costs.”
2- Definitions
2.1 The terms, words and phrases used in this Determination shall have the same meaning as are ascribed to them in the Telecommunications Law unless this Determination expressly provides for otherwise, or the context in which those terms, words and phrases are used in this Determination require it. For the puposes of this Determination:
2.1.1 Access Provider shall mean a Licensee who provides Indoor Mobile Site Sharing to the Access Seeker.
2.1.2 Access Seeker shall mean a Licensee who obtains Indoor Mobile Site Sharing from the Access Provider.
2.1.3 Licensees shall mean Etisalat and EITC. Language such as “a Licensee” shall be construed as to refer to either Etisalat or EITC as the context so requires.
2.1.4 Turnkey Cost shall mean the supplier costs related to the establishment of an indoor mobile site and any work or materials costs incurred by the Access Provider in installing any related equipment or components.
2.1.5 Engineering Supervision Activities shall mean the activities carried out by the Access Provider relating to overseeing the engineering aspects of indoor mobile site projects.
2.1.6 Lead Operator’s Fee shall mean a charge for the management activities that the Access Provider performs before and after the implementation of an indoor mobile site project. This excludes the activities covered by Engineering Supervision Activities.
2.1.7 ISS Operation and Maintenance Fee Shall mean the fee charged by the Access Provider to the Access Seeker for activities related to Operations and Maintenance of an indoor mobile site.
2.1.8 ISS upgrade Fee shall mean the fee charged by the Access Provider to the Access Seeker for the upgrade of an indoor mobile site.
2.1.9 ISS Upgrade Lead Operator’s Fee shall mean a charge for the management activities performed by the Access Provider before and after the implementation of an upgrade of an indoor mobile site project.
3. History
3.1 An extended period of negotiations between the Licensees failed to result in agreement on the charges for indoor mobile site sharing. As a consequence, the TRA decided to intervene to establish charges for both active and passive indoor mobile site sharing.
3.2 On 13 March 2016, the TRA issued a Closed Consultation Document entitled “indoor Mobile Site Sharing Charges” which invited comments from Licensees on the TRA’s proposed approaches to deriving the charges and defining the relevant cost inputs.
3.3 On 16 May 2016, the TRA exchanged for further review and comment the initial responses received from Licensees on the Consultation.
3.4 Having given due consideration to the comments received from the Licensees, the TRA hereby makes the following Determination.
4. The Determination
4.1 Having considered the provisions of Federal Law by Decree No. (3) of 2003, as amended, and the TRA’s Regulatory Framework, taking into consideration the inputs provided by the Licensees, the TRA hereby determines the Mobile Site Sharing Charges applicable to indoor mobile sites as follows.

Sharing of Passive Indoor Mobile Sites
4.3 The annual charges for sharing passive indoor sites shall be AED 236 per antenna.
4.4 The minimum amount the Access Seeker shall pay to the Access Provider for sharing a passive indoor mobile site shall be AED 15,000 per project per year.
Royalty charges
4.5 Any applicable revenue-based royalty charges shall be added to the charges specified above in relation to both active and passive sites.
Retrospective application
4.6 This Determination and the charges for site sharing which are referenced herein shall be applied retrospectively to cover all relevant existing indoor mobile site sharing arrangements, and shall remain in force until the TRA determines otherwise.
5. Effective Date
5.1 This Determination shall become effective upon issuance.
6. Acknowledgement
6.1 Each Licensee shall notify the TRA in writing of receipt of this Determination within one (1) business day of the date it is received.
