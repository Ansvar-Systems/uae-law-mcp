قرار مجلس إدارة الهيئة الاتحادية للرقابة النووية رقم 5
صادر بتاريخ 16/02/2024
الموافق فيه 6/شعبان/1445هـ
بشأن منح شركة الإمارات للطاقة النووية ش.م.ع. الترخيص لاختيار موقع تشييد مرافق نووية
مجلس إدارة "الهيئة الاتحادية للرقابة النووية" ("الهيئة"):
بعد الاطلاع على:
- المرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (6) لسنة 2009 في شأن الاستعمالات السلمية للطاقة النووية ("القانون")؛
- قانون إمارة أبوظبي رقم (8) لسنة 2021 بشأن شركة الإمارات للطاقة النووية "شركة مساهمة عامة"؛
- قرار مجلس الوزراء رقم (47م/1و) لسنة 2024 بتجديد تشكيل مجلس إدارة الهيئة الاتحادية للرقابة النووية؛
- قرار مجلس الوزراء رقم (2/9و) لسنة 2020 باعتماد دليل حوكمة مجالس الإدارة في الحكومة الاتحادية؛
- قرار مجلس الوزراء رقم (12/6و) لسنة 2021 بطلب استثناء الهيئة الاتحادية للرقابة النووية من تطبيق بعض أحكام دليل حوكمة مجالس الإدارة في الحكومة الاتحادية المعتمد بموجب قرار مجلس الوزراء رقم (2/9و) لسنة 2020؛
- قرار مجلس إدارة الهيئة رقم (15) لسنة 2021 بالتعديل رقم 4 للائحة الداخلية المنظمة لاجتماعات مجلس إدارة الهيئة الاتحادية للرقابة النووية؛ و
- "طلب ترخيص اختيار موقع مقدم من شركة الإمارات للطاقة النووية ش.م.ع، والتي تشكل طلب الحصول على ترخيص لممارسة النشاط الخاضع للرقابة المنصوص عليه في المادة 25 (1) من القانون، وهو اختيار الموقع لتشييد مرفق نووي؛ و
بناءً على مراجعة وتوصية مدير عام الهيئة لمجلس إدارة الهيئة عملاً بالمادة 15 (5) من القانون وبناءً على موافقة مجلس إدارة الهيئة خلال اجتماعه رقم (2) لسنة 2024 المنعقد في 16 فبراير 2024؛
قرّر ما يلي:
المادة الأولى
عملاً بالمواد 5، 6، 23، 24، 25، 28، و 32 من القانون، منح شركة الإمارات للطاقة النووية ش.م.ع "ترخيص لاختيار موقع تشييد مرافق نووية" (ترخيص رقم: FANR/NF/2024/01) كما هو وارد في الملحق 1 بهذا القرار.
المادة 2
يدخل هذا القرار حيز التنفيذ اعتباراً من تاريخ إصداره ويُنشر في الجريدة الرسمية.

Resolution No. (5) of 2024 of the Board of Management of the Federal Authority for Nuclear Regulation
Granting Emirates Nuclear Energy Company PJ.S.C
the Licence of the Selection of a Site for
Construction of Nuclear Facilities
The Board of Management of the Federal Authority for Nuclear Regulation ("FANR"):
Having considered:
• Federal Law by Decree No. (6) of 2009 Concerning the Peaceful Uses of Nuclear Energy (the "Law");
• Abu Dhabi Law No. (8) of 2021 Concerning Emirates Nuclear Energy Company "Public Joint Stock Company";
• Cabinet Resolution No. (47m/1g) of 2024 on the Renewal of the Formation of the Board of Management of the Federal Authority for Nuclear Regulation;
• Cabinet Resolution No. (2/9g) of 2020 on the Guide to Board Governance in the UAE Federal Government;
- Cabinet Resolution No. (12/6g) of 2021 Concerning Request to Exempt the Federal Authority for Nuclear Regulation from the Enforcement of Some of the Provisions of the Guide to Board Governance in the UAE Federal Government, Approved Pursuant to the Cabinet Resolution No. (2/9g) of 2020;
- FANR's Board of Management Resolution No. (15) of 2021 on Amendment No. 4 of the By-Laws Regulating the Meetings of the Board of Management of the Federal Authority for Nuclear Regulation; and
- "Application for Site Selection Licence" submitted by Emirates Nuclear Energy Company PJ.S.C. constituting the application for a licence to conduct the regulated activity stated in Article 25(1) of the Law, namely selection of a site for the construction of a nuclear facility; and
Based on the review and recommendation of FANR's Director General provided to FANR's Board of Management pursuant to Article 15(5) of the Law and based on FANR's Board of Management approval during its meeting No. (02) of 2024 held on 16 February 2024,
Has decided the following:
Article 1
Pursuant to Articles 5, 6, 23, 24, 25, 28 and 32 of the Law, to grant to Emirates Nuclear Energy Company P J.S.C. 'Licence for the Selection of a Site for the Construction of Nuclear Facilities' (Licence No. FANR/NF/2024/001) as provided in Annex 1 to this Resolution.
Article 2
This Resolution shall come into force as of the date of its issuance and shall be published in the Official Gazette.
Issued in Abu Dhabi
Date: 16 February 2024
Corresponding to: 06 Shaban 1445H
Abdulla Al Suwaidi Chairman
Licence No: FANR/NF/2024/001
Licence for the Selection of a Site for the Construction of
Nuclear Facilities
Under the powers provided in Articles (5), (6) and (28)(3)(a) of the Federal Law by Decree No. 6 of 2009 Concerning the Peaceful Uses of Nuclear Energy (the Law), and having considered that the proposed activities of Emirates Nuclear Energy Company P.J.S.C. comply with the requirements of the Law and applicable regulations of the Federal Authority for Nuclear Regulation (the Authority), the Authority, having considered a graded approach to the authorisation of Regulated Activities based on Safety, hereby grants this Licence for the selection of a site for the Construction of the Nuclear Facilities that will contain Nuclear Reactor(s) (the Licence) to:
Emirates Nuclear Energy Company P.J.S.C. (the Licensee)
being a public joint stock company (industrial licence No: IN-1002171) established pursuant to the Abu Dhabi Law No. 8 of 2021 on Emirates Nuclear Energy Company "Public Joint Stock Company (P.J.S.C)".
This Licence authorises the Licensee to conduct the following Regulated Activity set forth in Article 25(1) of the Law:
Selection of a site for the Construction of a Nuclear Facility
as specified in Schedule of 1 of this Licence. The Licence conditions are set forth In Schedule 2. The description of the Regulated Activity is provided in the Licence application documents referenced in Schedule 3. Schedule 4 provides interpretation of certain terms used in this Licence.
This Licence shall remain in force until:
a) it is suspended or revoked by the Authority; or
b) it is amended by the Authority; or
c) it is surrendered by the Licensee, subject to acceptance by the Authority; or
d) the Authority grants a licence for the Construction of the Nuclear Facility at the selected site.
This Licence is granted without prejudice to any future decision of the Authority regarding the following:
a) preparation of a site for the Construction of the Nuclear Facilities that will contain Nuclear Reactor(s); or
b) Construction of the Nuclear Facilities that will contain Nuclear Reactor(s).
Issued in Abu Dhabi
16 February 2024
Abdulla Al Suwaidi
Chairman of the Board of Management of
the Federal Authority

This Licence comprises this page, the preceding pages and the following schedules, which together form an integral part of the Licence:
Schedule 1: (1 page) Regulated Activities Authorised
Schedule 2: (1 page) Licence Conditions
Schedule 3: (1 page) Application Documents
Schedule 4: (1 page) Interpretation.
Any reference to 'schedule' in the Licence means a reference to a schedule of this Licence.
Schedule 1
Regulated Activities Authorised
1- This Licence authorises the selection of a site in the State for the Construction of the Nuclear Facilities that will contain Nuclear Reactor(s), which shall include only the regional analysis and identification of potential sites; screening of potential sites and identification of candidate sites; and evaluation of candidate sites so as to characterize fully the site-specific conditions pertinent to the Safety and security of a Nuclear Facility and activities reasonably associated with the foregoing.
2- The Licensee is required to obtain all necessary Licence(s) from the Authority for the conduct of any other Regulated Activity.
3- The Licensee shall obtain all necessary licences, permits and authorisations from other government entities for the conduct of any Regulated Activities authorised by the Authority under this Licence and update the Authority on any change relating to the aforementioned licences, permits and authorisations.
Schedule 2
Licence Conditions
This Licence is subject to and the Licensee shall comply with the Licence conditions specified and incorporated below:
1- The Licensee shall conduct the Regulated Activity authorised by this Licence in accordance with:
a) the Law, and any amendments thereto;
b) any applicable regulations issued by the Authority pursuant to the Law;
c) the terms and conditions of this Licence; and
d) the documents referenced in and/or submitted with the application for this Licence (the Application Documents).
2- The Licensee shall notify the Authority of any modification to Safety-related aspects of the Application Documents at least thirty (30) days prior to their implementation. Such modifications shall be subject to the Authority's re-assessment and approval.
3- The Licensee shall provide the Authority with a plan and timetable for specific activities to be undertaken under the general authority of this Licence at least thirty (30) days prior to such activities commencing.
4- The Licensee shall provide the Authority with access to any place, to any document or to any authorised person, and shall provide all assistance necessary to enable the Authority to conduct Inspections in accordance with Articles (5), (35) and (36) of the Law.
5- The Licensee shall provide to the Authority within one (1) month of the end of each calendar year, a report on the Regulated Activity carried out under this Licence during the previous year.
6- The Licensee shall retain any Safety-related records during the conduct of the Regulated Activity authorised by the Authority under this Licence during the lifetime of the Nuclear Facility.
7- The Licensee shall take into consideration the potential impact of climate change on the candidate site related hazards when performing the authorised Regulated Activities.
Schedule 3
Application Documents
1- Emirates Nuclear Energy Company Public Joint Stock Company - Application for Site Selection Licence (letter ref. no.: ENA-FANR-23-0061L, dated 27 September 2023).
Schedule 4
Interpretation
1- Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Licence shall have the meaning ascribed to them in Article (1) of the Federal Law by Decree No. 6 of 2009 Concerning the Peaceful Uses of Nuclear Energy.
