قرار رقم 21 لسنة 2009
صادر بتاريخ 05/08/2009م.
بشأن إعتماد وإصدار السياسة التنظيمية بشأن تكاليف تطبيق خدمة نقل الأرقام
الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الاتصالات
- بعد الاطلاع على المرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (3) لسنة 2003 في شأن تنظيم قطاع الاتصالات وتعديلاته،
- وعلى قرار اللجنة العليا للإشراف على قطاع الاتصالات رقم (3) لسنة 2004 بإصدار اللائحة التنفيذية للمرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (3) لسنة 2003 في شأن تنظيم قطاع الاتصالات والقرارات المعدلة لها،
وبناءا على ما عرضه مدير عام الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الاتصالات وموافقة مجلس ادارة الهيئة،
تقرر:
المادة الأولى
إعتماد وإصدار السياسة التنظيمية لتكاليف تطبيق خدمة نقل الأرقام كما هي مبينة في المرفق.
المادة 2
يعمل بهذا القرار إعتبارا من تاريخ صدوره وينشر في الجريدة الرسمية.

هيئة تنظيم الاتصالات

1. Legal Reference, Definitions and Interpretation
1.1 Article 14(8) of Federal Law by Decree no 3 of 2003 regarding the Organization of the Telecommunications Sector grants the TRA the power, inter alia, to issue regulations, orders, resolutions and procedures in relation to Mobile Number Portability.
1.2. The TRA has issued the Regulatory Policy for Number Portability and the Mobile Number Portability Instructions.
1.3 Paragraph 4.6 of the Mobile Number Portability Instructions provides that:
"the TRA shall from time to time, in its absolute discretion either:
4.6.1. recover from the Licensees (and if applicable, from any New Licensees);or
4.6.2 require payment in advance from the Licensees (and if applicable, from any New Licensees),
the amount of any costs, charges, fees and expenses incurred directly or indirectly by the TRA as a result of the introduction of MNP into the UAE, including any costs, charges, fees, and expenses incurred by the TRA under the MNP Contract. The TRA may do this through any mechanism it sees fit, including but not limited to the imposition of MNP Regulatory Fees.
The Licensees (and if applicable, any New Licensees) shall pay any MNP Regulatory Fees strictly as provided for by the TRA."
1.4 All words, terms and phrases used in this Regulatory Policy shall have the meanings ascribed to them, if any, in the Federal Law by Decree no 3 of 2003 regarding the Organization of the Telecommunications Sector or the TRA's Mobile Number Portability Instructions, from time to time as the case may be, or any replacement for either of them, unless otherwise provided for in this Regulatory Policy.
1.5 This Regulatory Policy should be read in conjunction with the Regulatory Policy for Number Portability and the Mobile Number Portability Instructions, PROVIDED THAT in the event of any inconsistency between the provisions of either or both of those instruments and this Regulatory Policy, this Regulatory Policy shall prevail as regards those matters the subject of it.
2. Scope
2.1 This Regulatory Policy sets out the details of the MNP Regulatory Fees.
2.2 Through the imposition of the MNP Regulatory Fees, the TRA is recovering the amount of costs, charges, fees and expenses incurred directly or indirectly by the TRA as a result of the introduction of MNP into the UAE, including any costs, charges, fees, and expenses incurred by the TRA under the MNP Contract.
2.3 The MNP Regulatory Fees are payable by the Licensees at the time and in the manner described in paragraph "3. The Policy" of this Regulatory Policy.
3. The Policy
3.1 Subject to paragraph 3.6, the MNP Regulatory Fees for the first five years following the date of this Regulatory Policy shall be payable in installments, each of which installments is set out in Annexure 1 to this Regulatory Policy (the "Installments").
3.2 Each of the Licensees shall pay each of the Installments within 15 calendar days of receipt of an invoice for the same issued by the TRA.
3.3 The TRA shall use its best endeavors to issue an invoice to each of the Licensees for the first Installment (being Installment 1 as set out in Annexure 1 to this Regulatory Policy) within 7 calendar days of this Regulatory Policy.
3.4 The TRA shall use its best endeavors to issue an invoice in respect of each other Installment (being Installments 2 to 5 as set out in Annexure 1 to this Regulatory Policy), no later than 15 calendar days prior to the date of commencement of the year in respect of which the relevant Installment is to be paid (as set out In Annexure 1 to this Regulatory Policy).
3.5 While the TRA will use its best endeavours to issue the invoices at the times referred to in paragraphs 3.3 and 3.4 above, if the TRA issues those invoices at any other time, the Licensees will still be obliged to pay those invoices within 15 calendar days of receipt of them.
3.6 Notwithstanding anything else in this Regulatory Policy the TRA reserves the right to, at any time, issue invoices for an MNP Regulatory Fee (other than the Installments), to recover the amount of any costs, charges, fees and expenses incurred directly or indirectly by the TRA as a result of the introduction and implementation of MNP into the UAE, which have not be accounted for in the calculation of the Installments.
4. Effective Date
This Regulatory Policy shall come into effect on the day that it is issued by the TRA.
5. Publication
The TRA reserves the right to, in its absolute discretion, publish this Regulatory Policy. Until the TRA exercises its discretion to do so, the Licensees shall regard it as confidential as between the TRA and each of the Licensees.
6. Revocation
The TRA reserves the right to replace, modify or reissue this Regulatory Policy at any time as it sees fit.
