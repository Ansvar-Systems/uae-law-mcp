Determination No. (1) 2017
CSI Link and WLL service charges
Version 1.0
Issue Date: 15 March 2017
1- Legal background
1.1 Article 12 of Federal Law by Decree No. (3) of 2003 regarding the organization of the Telecommunications Sector, as amended (the “Telecommunications Law”) established the TRA as the, “...competent body to oversee the telecommunications sector in the State and all Licensees...”
1.2 Article 14 of the Law stipulates that the TRA shall have the competence to issue regulations, instructions, decisions and rules “...regulating and ensuring competition in the telecommunications sector...”.
1.3 In Determination No (1) of 2012, Competition Assessment, dated 29 March 2012, the TRA determined that Etisalat and du have respective unilateral Market Power in the Relevant Markets WM8A and WM8B for wholesale terminating segments of fixed connectively services in their respective areas of the UAE where they provide direct fixed network connections to Customers. The TRA also determined that Etisalat has unilateral Market Power in the Relevant Market WM8C for wholesale trunk segments of fixed connectivity services.
1.4 Article 6.1 of the ex ante Competition Safeguards states that “Any Entities designated as having Market Power in a Relevant Market may be subject to ex ante obligations additional to those in place under the Regulatory Framework at the time of designation”.
1.5 The capitalised terms, words and phrases used in this determination (the “Determination”) shall have the same meaning as is ascribed to them in the Telecommunications Law, the ex ante Competition Safeguards and the Guidelines on Market Definition and Competition Assessment, Version 1.0 issued 30 September 2010 unless this Determination expressly provides for otherwise, or the context in which those terms, words and phrases are used in this Determination or Annexure requires otherwise.
2- History
2.1 On 29 March 2012, for the purpose of imposing remedies, the TRA issued the Competition Assessment Determination which assessed the Market Power of Licensees in Relevant Markets for Telecommunication Services and products in the UAE.
2.2 Based on the finding of Market Power in a number of Relevant Markets, the TRA published a consultation on remedies to be imposed on legacy Wholesale Leased Line (WLL) services composed of trunk (part of Market WM8C) and terminating (part of Markets WM8A and WM8B) segments and Customer Sited Interconnection (CSI) Link services on 14 June 2016 to provide stakeholders with an opportunity to comment on the remedies it proposes in order to mitigate the Market Power therein. On 27 October 2016 the TRA published a second consultation about the implementation of those remedies.
2.3 Following the closures of these consultations, the TRA received comments from the following parties:
• Emirates Telecommunications Corporation (“Etisalat”), and
• Emirates Integrated Telecommunications Company (“du”).
3- The Determination
3.1 Having reviewed and, where appropriate, taken into account relevant comments received during the consultation phases, the TRA hereby issues this Determination. In this Determination the TRA sets out the prices for CSI Link and WLL services and related provisions.
3.2 For the purpose of this Determination, the CSI Link and WLL services are defined as follows:
• CSI Link services are those provided in accordance with Schedule 3 of the Interconnection Agreement (ICA); a transmission service for conveying voice traffic between Etisalat’s and du’s networks with link capacities equal to those set out in Table 1 below.
• WLL services are those provided in accordance with Schedule 5 of the ICA, a service for the provision of dedicated leased lines/circuits, which are provided from any point “A” to any point “B” within the UAE with link capacities equal to those set out in Table 2 below.

3.4 Distances to which distance dependent charges are applied shall be measured and documented transparently on the basis of the underlying road network using the most direct route between two points. For the purpose of measuring the length for the CSI Link services, the points from which the measurements are taken are the locations of the respective POIs and fixed/mobile switches in Etisalat’s network. For WLL services, these points are the locations of the access nodes (e.g. OLT) to which the terminating segments are connected (or POI in the case that a WLL service is used to establish a retail leased line between the Licensees’ respective networks).
3.5 For any CSI Link or WLL service with linear distance below or equal to 40km the leased line distance shall not exceed a length of 1.35 × the linear distance. In the interest of ensuring that access is provided as quickly as possible, access seekers shall be able to opt for having the distance measured using this factor or the actual road length.
4- Effective Date
4.1 This Determination shall become effective upon issuance.
5- Acknowledgement
5.1 Each Licensee shall notify the TRA in writing of receipt of this Determination within one (1) business day of the date it is received.
