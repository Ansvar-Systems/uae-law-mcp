قرار رقم 55
صادر بتاريخ 18/12/2023م.
بشأن اعتماد مواصفات شكل علامة الثقة الإماراتية لخدمات الثقة المعتمدة
رئيس مجلس إدارة الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الاتصالات والحكومة الرقمية،،،،
- بعد الاطلاع على المرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (3) لسنة 2003 في شان تنظيم قطاع الاتصالات ولائحته التنفيذية وتعديلاتهما،
- وعلى المرسوم بقانون اتحادي رقم (46) لسنة 2021 بشأن المعاملات الإلكترونية وخدمات الثقة ولائحته التنفيذية،
- وبناءً على ما عرضه مدير عام الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الاتصالات والحكومة الرقمية، وموافقة مجلس إدارة الهيئة في اجتماعه المنعقد بتاريخ 18 ديسمبر 2023م، وعلى مذكرة الموافقة المرفوعة إليه من إدارة الهيئة،
قررنا ما يلي:
المادة الاولى
يعتمد بموجب هذا القرار "مواصفات شكل علامة الثقة الإماراتية لخدمات الثقة المعتمدة»" المرفقة بهذا القرار.
المادة 2
تكلف إدارة الهيئة باتخاذ التدابير والإجراءات اللازمة لحماية علامة الثقة الإماراتية، بما في ذلك تسجيلها لدى الجهات المختصة في الدولة، بما يمنع استخدامها بشكل غير مشروع.
المادة 3
يعمل بهذا القرار اعتباراً من تاريخ صدوره، وينشر في الجريدة الرسمية.
صدر بتاريخ 18 ديسمبر2023م.

The specifications relating to the form of the UAE trust mark for qualified trust services
Issue Date: 18 Dec. 2023
Article 1 - Definitions
The terms, words, and phrases used in this Resolution shall have the same meaning as are ascribed to them in the Federal Decree-Law No. (46) of 2021 on Electronic Transactions and Trust Services, unless this Resolution expressly provides for otherwise, or the context in which those terms, words and phrases are used in this Resolution requires otherwise. For the purposes of this Resolution, the following terms and words shall have the meanings ascribed to them below:
“State”: the United Arab Emirates;
“The Authority”: the Telecommunications and Digital Government Regulatory Authority (“the TDRA”);
“Board”: the Board of Directors of the Authority;
“Law": the Federal Decree-Law No. (46) of 2021 on Electronic Transactions and Trust Services;
“Executive Regulation”: the Federal Executive Regulation No. (28) of 2023;
“Trust Service Provider” (TSP): a Licensee authorized by the Authority, in accordance with the Law and its Executive Regulation, to provide one or more Trust Services;
“Qualified Trust Service Provider” (QTSP): a TSP granted the qualified status by the Authority to provide Trust Services and Qualified Trust Services accordingly;
“Trust Service” (TS): Electronic services specified under Article 17(1) of the Law, which a Trust Service Provider is authorized to provide as per the License granted thereto;
“Qualified Trust Services” (QTS): Electronic services specified under Article 17(2) of the Law, which a Qualified Trust Service Provider is authorized to provide as per the License granted thereto;
Article 2 - UAE Trust Mark
1- The UAE trust mark for Qualified Trust Services shall be in any of the forms shown in Annexe I without prejudice to the provisions laid down in Article 3.
2- The Authority shall made available on its website the forms of the UAE trust mark for Qualified Trust Services shown in Annexe I in various appropriate formats for use by Qualified Trust Service Providers.
Article 3 - Colours
1- The reference colours for the UAE trust mark for qualified trust services shall be:
1.1- Colour GOLD, Pantone 872C:
1.1.1- R: 161 G: 133 B: 96
1.1.2- C: 31 M: 40 Y: 60 K: 21
1.2- Colour GREY, Pantone Cool Gray 10
1.2.1- R: 99 G: 102 B: 106
1.2.2- C: 57 M: 46 Y: 40 K: 25
2- The UAE trust mark for qualified trust services may only be used in black and white if it is not practical to use colour. In such case, the colour grey referred to in paragraph 1 of this article shall be replaced by the colour black (R:0, G:0, B:0) and the colour gold referred to in paragraph 1 of this article shall be replaced by the colour white (R:255, G:255, B:255).
3- If the UAE trust mark for qualified trust services is used on a dark background, it may be used in negative format using the same background colour.
4- If the UAE trust mark for qualified trust services is used in colour on a coloured background, that makes it difficult to see, a delimiting outer line around the UAE trust mark for qualified trust services may be used to improve contrast with the background colours.
Article 4 - Resolution
1- The UAE trust mark for qualified trust services shall have a minimum size that ensures preservation of visual attributes and key forms, but its size shall not be less than 64 x 85 pixels 150 dpi.
Article 5- Resolution
1- The UAE trust mark for qualified trust services shall be used in a manner that allows clear indication of qualified services that the trust mark pertains to.
2- The UAE trust mark for qualified trust services may be associated with graphical or textual elements clearly indicating qualified trust services it is used for, under the condition that they do not change the nature of the UAE trust mark for qualified trust services, nor alter the link with the UAE trusted list referred to under Article (27) of the Law.
Article 6- Publication
This Resolution shall be published in the Official Gazette and shall come into force from the date of its issuance
