صادر بتاريخ 19/12/2016م.
بشأن إعتماد سياسة استخدام موارد الأرقام
رئيس مجلس إدارة الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الإتصالات،
بعد الإطلاع على المرسوم بقانون إتحادي رقم (3) لسنة 2003 في شأن تنظيم قطاع الإتصالات ولائحته التنفيذية وتعديلاتهما؛
وعلى القرار رقم (45) لسنة 2015 بشأن الموافقة على إصدار سياسة الخطة الوطنية للأرقام (5.3) وسياسة رسوم تخصيص واستخدام موارد الأرقام (2.4)،
وبناءً على ما عرضه مدير عام الهيئة العامة لتنظيم قطاع الإتصالات، وموافقة مجلس إدارة الهيئة في اجتماعه المنعقد بتاريخ 7 ديسمبر 2016، على مذكرة الموافقة المرفوعة إليه من الهيئة،
قررنا ما يلي:
المادة الأولى-
يُعتمد بموجب هذا القرار الإصدار رقم (2.5) من سياسة استخدام موارد الأرقام المرفقة بهذا القرار.
المادة 2-
يُلغى أي نص في أي قرار آخر إلى المدى الذي يتعارض فيه وأحكام هذا القرار والسياسة المرفقة به.
المادة 3-
يُعمل بهذا القرار اعتباراً من تاريخ صدوره، ويُنشر في الجريدة الرسمية.
صدر في دبي بتاريخ 19 ديسمبر 2016م.

Regulatory Policy
Fees for the Allocation and Use of Number Resources
Version 2.5
Issue Date: 7 Dec 2016
Based on the Federal Decree by Law No.3 of 2003, its Executive Order and directives by the Supreme Committee, the Telecommunication Regulatory Authority (TRA) is pleased to announce this Policy on the fees it will charge for the allocation and use of Numbering Resources.
ARTICLE 1- Definitions:
Application Fee: The fee payable by Licensees and others that may apply for numbering resources when they submit their application for an allocation of numbering resources.
Annual fee: The fee payable by Licensees and others allocated numbering resources on the first of January each year, or when the numbering resource is allocated to them (if this is after the first of January).
Authority: The General Authority for Regulating the Telecommunication Sector (the Telecommunications Regulatory Authority).
Government: The government of the State of the United Arab Emirates.
International Regulations: Regulations, rules, specifications, definitions, recommendations or agreements ratified by UAE at the international level.
Licensees: Any telecom operator who provides telecommunication services licensed by the Supreme Committee pursuant to the provisions of the Federal Law by Decree and its Executive Order.
National Numbering Plan: Number Plan for UAE issued by TRA in accordance with the International Telecommunications Union- Telecommunications Standardization Sector Recommendation E. 164
Numbering Resources: Prefixes, individual numbers, numbering ranges and codes used by the Licensees in operating their telecommunications networks
Ported Numbers: An individual number or block of Numbering Resource that have been allocated (as part of a block) by the TRA to a Licensee and then transferred to another Licensee in accordance with the Policy for Number Portability.
Article 2
Based on Paragraph No.8 of Article 14 of the Federal Law by Decree No.3 of 2003 as amended, the Telecommunication Regulatory Authority (TRA) has the power to issue regulations, orders, procedures and resolutions for the allocation of telephone numbers, numbering plans and number portability
Based on Paragraphs 3 and 4 of Article 17 of the Federal Law by Decree No.3 of 2003 as amended, the financial resources of the Authority may be derived from such fees as may be charged by the Board for any services provided by the Authority or the authorizations or approvals granted by the Authority pursuant to the provisions of this Federal Law by Decree, its Executive Order and decisions issued by the Board and any other revenues the Authority collects in pursuance of its activities and functions.
Based on Article 44 of the Federal Law by Decree No.3 of 2003 as amended, the Authority shall be responsible for allocating prefixes, private telephone numbers and numbering ranges to Licensees in accordance with the provisions of this Federal Law by Decree, its Executive Order and the conditions set by the Authority and approved by the Board.
Based on the directive of the Board on Fees for the Allocation and Use of Number Resources the TRA is obliged to establish a policy for the value of the fees to be charged.
ARTICLE 3
The TRA has determined a Fee to accompany each application for numbering resource to be 500 dirham.
No Application Fee is required for applications received from law enforcement entities relevant to law enforcement services, nor those to extend or remove a time condition attached to an earlier allocation of numbering resources that limit the time that the numbering resource could be used by the Licensee or others who are allocated numbering resources. However, applications to modify or remove all other conditions attached to earlier allocations of numbering resources will incur an Application Fee of 500 dirham.
Furthermore, the TRA has determined a fee of 500 dirhams for declaring a right to use Number to a customer, where a certificate is issued/ registered by the TRA for the benefit of that customer. This fee shall also be applicable in the case of any change request for the certificate.
ARTICLE 4
The Annual Fee payable in advance on the first day of January each year or on a prorata where the numbering resource is allocated by the TRA where this is after the first day of January and annually on the first day of January thereafter is as follows:
1- Numbering Resource for geographic services where the block of allocated numbers is decided by the TRA.0.4 Dhs per number allocated.
2- Numbering Resource for geographic services where the block of allocated numbers is requested by the Licensee, other corporate bodies or individuals.1.2 Dhs per number allocated.
3- Numbering Resource for Mobile services. 2 Dhs per number allocated.
4- Numbering Resource for value added network services except shared revenue, shared cost and FreePhone/ Toll Free services where the block of allocated numbers is decided by the TRA. 500 Dhs per number allocated.
5- Numbering Resource for value added network services except shared revenue, shared cost and FreePhone / Toll Free services where the block of allocated numbers is requested by the Licensee, other corporate bodies or individuals. 1,500 Dhs per number allocated.
6- Shared revenue services where the block of allocated numbers is decided by the TRA. 500 Dhs per number allocated.
7- Shared revenue services where the block of allocated numbers is requested by the Licensee, other corporate bodies or individuals. 1,500 Dhs per number allocated.
8- Shared cost services where the block of allocated numbers is decided by the TRA. 50 Dhs per number allocated.
9- Shared cost services where the block of allocated numbers is requested by the Licensee, other corporate bodies or individuals. 150 Dhs per number allocated.
10- Freephone / Toll free services where the block of allocated numbers is decided by the TRA. 50 Dhs per number allocated.
11- Freephone / Toll free services where the block of allocated numbers is requested by the Licensee, other corporate bodies or individuals. 150 Dhs per number allocated.
12- Numbering Resource from level ‘1’ where a call charge is made to the customer dialling the number. 75,000 Dhs per number allocated.
13- Codes for Carrier Selection and Carrier Pre-selection override services:
08 + 3 digit codes. 75,000 Dhs per number allocated.
14- Mobile Prefix (05Y). 5 Million Dhs.
15- Premium SMS Short Codes. 7,500 Dhs per number allocated.
16- Nomadic Services Numbers. 1.2 Dhs per number allocated.
17- M2M Services Numbers. 0.2 Dhs per Number allocated.
18- TETRA Services Numbers (except TETRA numbers used for law enforcement services). 2 Dhs per number allocated.
19- Integrated Message Handling System. 2 Dhs per number allocated.
ARTICLE 5
The TRA may and on its discrete decision undertake auctions for numbering resource when there is a demonstrable demand from Licensees and others for the allocation of the same numbering resource.
ARTICLE 6
Numbering Resources utilized by a Licensee before the issuance of Policy for “Fees for the Allocation and Use of Number Resources” version 1.0 issued on 24th January 2006 will incur a fee as if the resources were decided by the TRA as set in Article 4 of this policy.
ARTICLE 7
Where an operator makes a charge to a customer for the number, either as an explicit charge or as part of a subscription package, and the fee is judged by the Telecommunications Regulatory Authority to be excessive, the Authority will specify the price that can be charged.
ARTICLE 8
The TRA will adjust the fees charged to a Licensee to reflect any numbers that have been ported to another Licensee or ported from another Licensee. To ascertain the appropriate adjustment, Licensees must respond to any request from the TRA for information pertaining to Ported Numbers.
ARTICLE 9
The TRA will refuse to make further allocations of numbering resource and may withdraw an allocation of numbering resource from a Licensee or others allocated numbering resources when the Annual Fee is unpaid one calendar month after it becomes payable. The TRA may also impose financial penalties on late payments.
ARTICLE 10
Licensees and others allocated numbering resource shall supply to the TRA all information requested by the TRA to enable the TRA to undertake a review of this policy.
